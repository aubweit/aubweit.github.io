A small craft brewery in downtown McMinnville.

 

At DBB we believe that everyone can enjoy the magic of beer. From the homebrewers who try and the craft the best brews to the casual drinker who are looking for the best drinking experience, we want to help you find your way into the amazing craft beer culture. We have our in house brews for everyone to enjoy in our tap room, as well as supplies for those that wish to make it themselves. Our classes can teach you everything from beer basics to brewing your own masterpieces.

 

 

Chris Weitzel

Co-Owner/ Head Brewer

 

Chris is a journeyman electrician from McMinnville. He became obsessed with craft beer after having a Calfkiller Grassroots Ale on his 21st birthday. Two months later his wife, Becky, bought him all the equipment he needed to make his own. After 7+ years of homebrewing and a lot of encouragement from friends and family, he set about bringing better beer to the public by founding Depot Bottom Brewery. Chris likes to play with ingredients and styles to keep things interesting for craft beer enthusiasts, yet approachable for those that are new to the scene.

 

 

Becky Weitzel

Co-Owner/ Brewery Lab Technician

 

Becky has a B.S. in Physics from the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga. She has been assisting in the homebrewing process for 11+ years, learning the basics from a fellow physics student. She helped steer Chris into the craft beer world (she bought that first Grassroots Ale) and has been his staunchest supporter every step of the way. Becky enjoys researching Warren County history for beer names and has a mild obsession with yeast.
